⛵️ Sport Fundamentals

TL;DR — Sailing is a Racing sport (not ball sport). Race format = multiple boats on a course with marks. Unique: multiple competitors per "game," position-based scoring, no fixed time clock.

Race structure, scoring, game progress, and key sport-specific concepts for WSC

Race Format & Structure (America's Cup)

AC75 Format (LVC + Match)

  • Two boats race head-to-head (OneVsOne)
  • Match racing format: position/finishing order determines winner
  • Race course: windward-leeward gates with multiple mark roundings
  • Typically 4-6 legs per race (up, down, up, down...)
  • Race duration: 20-30 minutes (AC75 foiling at 40-50+ knots)
  • OCS (On Course Side): false start infringement

AC40 Format (Regattas)

  • Multiple boats (4-5 teams) racing simultaneously
  • Fleet racing format: all teams sail the same course
  • Scoring: 1st = 1 point, 2nd = 2 points... lowest score wins series
  • Race duration: 20-40 minutes
  • Important: Fleet racing needs multi-competitor game model
  • Links to DEV-270026 (OneVsMany/ManyVsMany)

Game Progress Structure

RaceIndividual gameGameSingle race = 1 WSC Game. 20-45 min duration.

Key Sailing Terms for WSC Operators

Content opportunities and use cases for Yacht Sailing broadcast clients

Broadcast Content Opportunities

  • Race Recap (60-90s) — Auto-generated highlight reel from each individual race. Start → Tacking duel → Mark Rounding → Lead Change → Finish. Perfect for social media.
  • Speed Highlight — Clip of highest-speed moment in race with speed overlay graphic. AC75 at 50+ knots is visually stunning. Short-form shareable content.
  • Penalty Drama — The moment a penalty is called + the spin execution. Race-defining, high emotion.
  • Start Line Highlights — Pre-start tactical maneuvering. Often the most strategic and dramatic part of the race for sailing experts.
  • Lead Change Compilation — All lead changes across a day of racing. Tells the story of who dominated.
  • Day in Review — Compiled highlights across all races on a single day. Multi-race content for evening broadcast recaps.
  • Team Performance Story — "Emirates Team NZ won 3 races today" — team-centric content for national/team fan bases.
  • Foiling Moments — Compilation of the most spectacular foiling sequences. Very visual, zero context needed. Instagram/TikTok ready.
  • Pre-race Atmosphere — Crowd, boats, conditions. Contextual content for broadcast opening segments.
  • Incident Report — Capsizes, gear failures, close calls. Rare but viral content with broad appeal beyond sailing fans.

Content Preferences

  • Speed and technology-focused highlights
  • Short clips for social sharing (30-60s ideal)

  • Stories format: perfect for pre-race to finish narrative
  • Multi-language demand (English, Italian, French, Spanish)
